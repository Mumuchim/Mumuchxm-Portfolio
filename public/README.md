# Static Assets

Place these files here before deploying:

- `logo.png`       — Your favicon (recommended 32×32 or 64×64)
- `og-preview.png` — Open Graph share image (recommended 1200×630)

These are referenced in `index.html` for the favicon and social media previews.
- `resume.pdf`     — Your resume (linked by the "RESUME ↓" button in the hero)
